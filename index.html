Our Process

  1. Introduction

    Say Hello Tell us about your project!

    We are a small team, so we like to start by having a quick phone call or meeting. Learning more about you and your industry, goals, audience, and desired outcomes is not only interesting to us, but it helps us build a better product for you.

    Whether you know exactly what you wantor only have a general concept in mindat this stage we're just looking for the basic details of your project.

    tl;dr I just want to get started! Say Hello

  2. Assessment

    Scope of Work Budget Outline Timeline Quote Agreement

    We understand that business insights, budgets and personal philosophies naturally structure the direction of most plans, so we aim to be as flexible as needed, once we know your goals, we put together a custom quote and timeline for an agreement.

  3. Project Management

    Collaboration and Strategy Collect Assets Feedback Checkpoints

    To start, we gather relevant assets from you, sign up for accounts, purchase licenses for any third-party services or products needed for project fulfillment.

    If you have content or preliminary concepts to share, we want them at this stage. If you have no idea what you'd like, that's okay, we will work through the details to help you with a strategy.

    As the project progresses, the management typically consist of emails, or if needed, short virtual or phone meetings to clarify direction or questions that come up during the design and development phases.

  4. Design & Development

    A Multidisciplinary Approach

    We've got this part! Using our years of experience and skill, we will design and develop your project.

    Design

    We use Adobe Creative Cloud Suite or Figma for most projects. The design can match established branding or fresh concepts can be created for new to the market material.

    Development

    Depending on requirements, web backends are constructed in WordPress, Symfony and/or Laravel, and front-end web projects are done in HTML 5, CSS 3, SCSS, ES6, or Typescript. Our code is clean, clear, and commented.

  5. Quality Assurance & Delivery

    Testing Approval Final File Transfer Archiving

    Essentially, we review or test the product in its complete form so it can be delivered to you.

    Once everything is approved, we proceed with either making the website live or turning over the final files. After the agreement is fulfilled, you'll have ownership of everything that was built, and if needed, restores are available we archive every project for up to two years.

  6. Consulting

    Per-Project Assistance

    Our clients tend to view us as an extension to their team, filling in as needed. We work closely to help identify gaps and gather requirements, so a solid strategy is outlined. Our experience helps us anticipate opportunities or problems early on, so you get a solid final product.

    NDAs are often a part of our agreements and are honored if requested.

    We're here to make the process easier. If you're looking for a web development partner to help you achieve your business goals, please contact us!
